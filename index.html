The Problem

FPV drones changed the economics of lethality.

By 2025, public reporting estimated drones were responsible for roughly 70% of casualties in Ukraine.

Low-cost drones now threaten armored platforms, fixed positions, and logistics chains at scale.

Defenders absorb high replacement costs while attackers iterate rapidly with commodity components.

Conventional protection stacks were not designed for persistent, distributed aerial attacks.

Source note: estimate derived from open-source 2025 battlefield reporting; figures vary by methodology and reporting period.
